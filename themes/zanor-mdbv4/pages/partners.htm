Employment is essential for incarcerated individuals to return to society. Jobs help with rehabilitation and create opportunities to start a new life.

Why hire someone who was formerly incarcerated? You get employees who are:

  • Dependable
  • Accountable
  • Available
  • Work Ready

Additional employer incentives:

  • Tax credit up to $2,400 for each hire
  • Federal Bonding program can provide insurance for employers
  • WORKReady! Certified Candidates
